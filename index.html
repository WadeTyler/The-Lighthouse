Welcome to the Lighthouse

Located where the Chesapeake Bay and Potomac River converge, The Lighthouse is a beach-chic hospitality destination on Maryland’s Western Shore. World-class restaurant, uniquely curated motel and an eclectic boutique…waterfront with stunning sunset views.

The Lighthouse is a recently refurbished 1940s-era fishing camp in St. Mary’s County, Maryland. We have brought this beloved property back to life with an artful interpretation of its restaurant, motel and pier, adding gardens both for food source and relaxation.
